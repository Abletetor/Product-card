Product Card Design

A product card is a condensed representation of an item available for purchase. It typically includes essential information such as product name, price, and a brief description. These cards often feature an image of the product to provide a visual representation for potential buyers. They serve as quick reference points for users browsing through a collection of items on an e-commerce platform. Product cards are designed to streamline the shopping experience by presenting key details in a concise and easily digestible format.
